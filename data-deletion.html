Account and Data Deletion

Last updated: July 4, 2026

You can request deletion of your Gemmi account and associated data by email. This includes Google Sign-In or Sign in with Apple account association, wallet records, referral records, generated image records, and related app data stored by Gemmi.

Request deletion by email

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email us at astardv@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject: Gemmi Account Deletion Request.
  3. Include the Google or Apple email address used to sign in to Gemmi. If you use Apple's private relay email, include that relay email if available.
  4. If available, include your referral code or wallet code to help us identify the account.

Data That May Be Deleted

  • Gemmi account identifier and Google-linked or Apple-linked profile information stored by Gemmi.
  • Wallet balance, coin transactions, reward history, and referral records.
  • Generated image records stored by Gemmi.
  • Uploaded images stored by Gemmi where they can be linked to your account.

Data That May Be Retained

Some information may be retained where required for security, fraud prevention, purchase or subscription verification, legal compliance, dispute resolution, backup integrity, or aggregated analytics that no longer identifies you personally.

Processing Time

We aim to process valid deletion requests within 30 days. We may ask for additional information if we cannot verify the account from the details provided.
